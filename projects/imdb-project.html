IMDb Redesign

"Bringing together people who are passionate about the world of motion pictures and animations, and helping them build a community to discuss, understand and explore the world of cinema more effectively."

  • Project's Motivation

    As a student studying HCI, I decided to take 'HCC 710 Graphic Design for Information Systems' as one of my subject. Through out the course, our professor guided us through various aspects of graphic design. As we finished different topics, we were tasked to implement what we had learned in the class. All this experience came together when we were asked to pick a website to redesign for our final leave-behind project. I decided to redesign, or rather reimagine IMDb.

    IMDb, although being owned by one of the biggest companies in the world seemed a bit outdated to me. The User Interface was not consistent, and most of the elements on the website seemed outdated. I wanted to explore all the different ways I could not just improve but also make an impact with my redesign.

  • Estimated Impact

    You see the world of cinematography and animation is all about storytelling. And at the heart of storytelling are the hearts and souls of the people who are watching it. Motion pictures are meant to connect with people. Bring them together. To share a feeling. To make a difference.

    And this is what i envisioned when i started working on this project. How to use this, seemingly unimaginably large resource like IMDb, to bring people close together.
