About me

I am Neeraj Gupta, a dedicated and detail-oriented graduate student in Human-Centered Computing at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC), specializing in Computer Supported Collaborative Work (CSCW).

I am actively working on a thesis in this field, exploring innovative solutions to enhance collaborative experiences. With a solid foundation in Computer Engineering, I bring a unique blend of technical skills and creative thinking to the table.

My experiences include redesigning student portals, creating impactful marketing materials for businesses, and leading data visualization projects. Proficient in tools like Figma, Illustrator, and Python, I excel in collaborative environments where I can apply my UX skills to create innovative solutions that enhance user experiences.
